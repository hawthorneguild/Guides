## Beyond 20 Settings

Beyond 20 is a Chrome add on that allows you to use your D&DBeyond character sheet to make rolls and send those rolls to other VTTs like Roll20 and Foundry. 

The chrome add-on can be found here:  [Beyond 20 Chrome Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/beyond-20/gnblbpbepfbfmoobegdogkglpbhcjofh?hl=en&pli=1)
