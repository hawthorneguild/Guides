Submit Your Monster

Share your homebrew creations with the Hawthorne community!

Follow these instructions to submit a homebrew monster that will be available for all Hawthorne DMs to use. Your creation will be added to the official Hawthorne Monster Compendium after review.

1

Create Your Statblock

Use the Statblock Builder to create your monster. The builder provides a user-friendly interface with all the fields you need for a proper D&D 5e stat block.

Open Statblock Builder

Tips:

  • Use the Preview button to see how your statblock will look
  • Make sure all required fields are filled in
  • When satisfied, click Download Markdown to save your work
2

Submit for Review

Once your statblock is ready, submit it to the Hawthorne community for review.

Go to #dm-requests on Discord

What to do:

  • Navigate to the #dm-requests channel on the Hawthorne Discord server
  • Upload the markdown file you downloaded from the Statblock Builder in your request
3

Make Revisions (If Needed)

If the review team requests changes, you can easily update your monster and resubmit.

How to revise:

  • Open the Statblock Builder
  • Click Load Markdown and select your original file
  • Make the requested changes
  • Click Download Markdown to save the updated version
  • Resubmit the new file in #dm-requests
