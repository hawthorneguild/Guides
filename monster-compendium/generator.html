Hawthorne Bestiary: Markdown Generator

Use this tool to make a 2024-compatible statblock for the Hawthorne Monster Compendium.

How to Use This Tool

Follow these steps to create and submit your monster:

  • Fill out the form: Complete all the fields in the "Form" tab. Note if you want to use headers in sections like Lore or Regional Effects, please use Heading 4 (####)
  • Preview your work: Click the "Preview" tab to see a live visual of your stat block. Any errors or suggestions will be listed at the top.
  • Download the file: Once you're satisfied, click the "Download Markdown" button. This will save a .md file to your computer.
  • Submit for review: Go to the #dm-requests channel on Discord and upload your .md file to have it added to the compendium.
  • To edit an existing monster: Click the "Load Markdown" button, select your saved .md file, and the form will be populated with its data.
